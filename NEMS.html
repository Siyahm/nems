ABOUT US

NAJATH ENGLISH MEDIUM SCHOOL was established in 1993 and
administered by Darunnajath Islamic Centre. The school is affiliated to
Central Board of Secondary Education, Delhi. The school is situated in a
peaceful atmosphere nearly half kilometer away from the heart of
Karuvarakundu.

FOUNDER

The history of Darunnajath ,in fact, is the life story of
the late Jb K.T. Manu Musliyar , the best-known
scholar and philanthropist who had taken service of
humanity his life’s mission and mantra. Though he is
greatly missed, Jb Manu Musliyar’s exemplary life,
characterised by such noble virtues as humility,
dedication and uncompromising moral principles
remain the real source of our strength and
inspiration. May Almighty bless his soul with eternal
bliss and peace!
